About AU ZURI

Au Zuri was founded to address the issues that disadvantaged communities confront, with a focus on rural populations, women, and young people. The group seeks to address important issues including poverty, gender-based violence, environmental problems, and public health difficulties by offering thorough education, support services, and long-term solutions. The organization's objective to empower and elevate marginalised communities through a comprehensive approach was enhanced as it expanded its reach to include programs centred on youth empowerment, agricultural efforts, water security projects, and climate change awareness. This is a non-profit organisation dedicated to cultural preservation, environmental sustainability, skills development, and community empowerment.


🌍 Vision

To build vibrant communities where culture, heritage and environmental responsibility thrive.


🎯 Mission

To empower individuals and communities through education, cultural programmes, environmental initiatives and sustainable development projects.


⭐ Core Values

  • Respect for Heritage
  • Community Empowerment
  • Environmental Stewardship
  • Sustainability
  • Integrity
  • Inclusivity
  • Gender Equality

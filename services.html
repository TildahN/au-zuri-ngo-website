Our Core Programmes

AU ZURI NGO provides a range of community-based programmes that focus on sustainability, empowerment, education, and health improvement.

🌍 Climate Change & Environmental Awareness

This programme focuses on educating communities about climate change, environmental protection, and sustainable living practices. Activities include awareness campaigns, tree planting initiatives, and environmental workshops that help communities understand how to protect natural resources.

  • Community environmental workshops
  • Tree planting campaigns
  • Climate change awareness training
  • Recycling and waste management education

💧 Water & Sanitation (WASH Programme)

Our Water and Sanitation programme supports communities in gaining access to clean water and promoting hygiene. We work to improve sanitation facilities and educate families on healthy hygiene practices to prevent disease.

  • Access to clean water initiatives
  • Hygiene awareness campaigns
  • Sanitation improvement projects
  • Community health education

👩 Gender-Based Violence & Women Empowerment

Au Zuri works to prevent gender-based violence and support survivors through awareness campaigns, counselling support, and empowerment programmes that help women gain independence and confidence.

  • GBV prevention awareness
  • Women's empowerment workshops
  • Skills development programmes
  • Support services for survivors

❤️ HIV & AIDS Awareness Programme

This programme promotes awareness, prevention, and support for individuals affected by HIV and AIDS. We provide education to youth and communities to reduce stigma and encourage healthy lifestyles.

  • HIV prevention education
  • Youth-friendly health services
  • Community awareness campaigns
  • Support and referral services

🌾 Agriculture & Food Security

Our agriculture programme teaches climate-smart farming methods that improve food production and nutrition. Communities learn how to grow crops sustainably and maintain household food security.

  • Climate-smart farming training
  • Home gardening projects
  • Nutrition education
  • Food sustainability workshops

📚 Youth Development & Education

This programme supports youth through educational assistance, mentorship, and life skills training. We also address teenage pregnancy, drug abuse, and school dropout challenges.

  • After-school support programmes
  • Life skills training
  • Career guidance workshops
  • Drug abuse awareness campaigns

🏺 Culture & Heritage Development

Au Zuri promotes cultural awareness and preservation of indigenous knowledge. We encourage women to take leadership roles in cultural development and community heritage projects.

  • Cultural leadership training
  • Heritage awareness programmes
  • Indigenous knowledge preservation
  • Community cultural events
